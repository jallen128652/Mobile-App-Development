Barbecue chicken consists of chicken parts or entire chickens that are barbecued, grilled or smoked. There are many global and regional preparation techniques. Barbecue chicken is often seasoned or coated in a spice rub, barbecue sauce, or both.

BBQ ribs are made of pork or beef ribs that are cooked using the low, indirect heat and smoke of a barbecue pit or the high, direct heat of a grill. Prior to cooking the ribs, a rub or marinade is often used to flavor and tenderize the meat.

Shish kebab or shish kebap is a popular meal of skewered and grilled cubes of meat.It is traditionally made of lamb[3] but there are also versions with various kinds of meat, poultry, or fish. In Turkey, shish kebab and the vegetables served are grilled separately.

Smoked sausage is a smoked meat dish consisting of sausage cooked via the indirect heat of woodsmoke. Sausages are meat products consisting of minced or ground meat and spices stuffed into a casing.

Tomahawk steak is a bone-in ribeye steak that has been trimmed to resemble the shape of a tomahawk, cut from the beef rib. This steak is not only great for grilling but also for pan-searing, broiling, or roasting.
